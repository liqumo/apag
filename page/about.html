About APAG

The APAG Group comprise of an executive committee and non executive committee and its members.


The committee consist of Chairman, Vice-Chairman, Secretary, Alternate Secretary and Treasurer and a group of committee members with specific roles and duties. Members were invited from various professional backgrounds and it is hoped that the group nurtures a varied list of professionals from all industries sector.


The APAG Group seek to drive its mission via the professionalism of its members and to ensure the reputation and branding of APAG.


Mission

APAG has as its central aim to promote understanding between cultures from the standpoint of a professional services network group, founded in Australia. This is achieved via providing forums for meetings and discussions and networking among different disciplines and cultures. In the main, it seeks to maintain a "East Meets West" perspective.


Its final aim, is hopefully to achieve an enhanced friendship and understanding between cultures, countries and to promote international trade flows.


Services

The following is the list of services provided by APAG:-

  • Hosting of networking functions
  • Delivering seminars to enhance technical and professional education
  • Provision of advertising medium for professional service providers
  • Provision of a "Welcoming Committee" services via eg "Selamat Datang" events (Malaysian flavour) for newcomers/business migrant to Australia
  • Counselling sessions for professionals
  • Provision of Secretariat to facilitate membership and enquiries
